Welcome to my Personal Homepage!

I am a recent graduate from Ahmedabad University, India, where I majored in Physics alongside a minor in Mathematics. I am currently located in Bengaluru.

Working alongside Dr. Samyaday Choudhury (Ahmedabad University, India) and in collaboration with Dr. Annalisa Calamida (Space Telescope Science Institute, USA), I carried out my undergraduate thesis work titled 'Study of White Dwarfs in the Globular Cluster NGC2808'.

Currently, I am a research intern at Ahmedabad University with the Astronomy & Astrophysics Group undergone Professor Samyaday Chowdhury.

My scientific interests include various stellar populations in Globular Clusters such as White Dwarf Stars and Blue Straggler Stars. Other than this, I am interested in understanding the evolution of single and binary stars. Recently, I have been curious to know about implications of black holes at the center of globular clusters. Nevertheless, I am not limited to these areas of research.

I grew up in Lucknow, India and attended La Martiniere College.

My hobbies include cycling, painting and writing documents in TeX.
